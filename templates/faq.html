Frequently asked questions (faq)

  1. What is Quizwiz?

    Quizwiz is an interactive quiz game website designed to test your knowledge on a wide range of topics while having fun.

  2. How do I play the quizzes?

    Playing quizzes on Quizwiz is easy. Simply select a quiz topic, answer the questions, and see how well you did at the end.

  3. Do I need to create an account to play?

    No, you can enjoy our quizzes without creating an account. However, having an account allows you to track your scores and compete with others on the leaderboard.

  4. Can I play on my mobile device?

    Yes, our website is responsive and can be enjoyed on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

  5. How do I reset my password?

    If you've created an account and need to reset your password, you can do so on the login page or on your profile page. Just follow the prompts as they come.

  6. Are there different difficulty levels for quizzes?

    Some quizzes may have varying levels of difficulty. You can choose the one that suits your knowledge level.

  7. How do I contact customer support?

    You can contact our customer support team, just visit the contact us page.

  8. Are there any prizes for high scores?

    While we don't offer prizes, you can compete with others and earn a spot on our leaderboard.

  9. Can I suggest a quiz topic?

    We welcome suggestions for new quiz topics! Please visit our Contact Us page to share your ideas with us.

  10. Is Quizwiz free to use?

    Yes, Quizwiz is completely free to use, and you can enjoy unlimited quizzes.

  11. Are there any age restrictions for using Quizwiz?

    Quizwiz is intended for users of all ages. However, parental guidance is advised for younger users, as some quiz topics may not be suitable for children.

  12. Can I create my own quizzes on Quizwiz?

    Currently, we do not offer the option for users to create their own quizzes, but we're continuously adding new quiz topics for your enjoyment.
